8 Extending Video

 (require video/surface) package: video

Video also has basic syntax for creating new types of producers, transitions, and filters.

This API is very experimental and WILL change without notice. Proceed with caution. The documentation only lists the forms available, as the details themselves will change.

define-producer ->producer defproducer define-transition ->transition deftransition
