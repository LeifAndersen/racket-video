7 Extended Video Lib

 (require video/lib) package: video

procedure

(converted-video? video) → boolean?

  video : video?
Determines if the video has been compiled (using convert-to-mlt! or render).

video is the video to test.

procedure

(producer-length/uneditied producer) → number?

  producer : (and/c video? converted-video?)
Similar to producer-length. However, it returns the unedited length of the producer. This is the length of the producer if it was not shortened.

producer is the video who’s length will be tested.

procedure

(playlist-clip-length playlist index)

 → exact-nonnegative-integer?
  playlist : playlist?
  index : exact-nonnegative-integer?
Gives the total play time of the index clip in the given playlist. Compare to producer-length.

procedure

(playlist-clip-start playlist index)

 → exact-nonnegative-integer?
  playlist : playlist?
  index : exact-nonnegative-integer?
Returns the length of the index clip in the given playlist. Compare to playlist-clip-length.

procedure

(video-type video) → identifier?

  video : video?
Returns an identifier for the type of video as defined in video/core. This function in particular is likely to get removed!!!
